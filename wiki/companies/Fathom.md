# Fathom

Meeting intelligence company and [[Grain]]'s closest direct competitor. Larger and growing faster than Grain as of May 2026, but with a different strategic focus.

## Update 2026-05-15 from [[2026-05-15_carlos-almonte-and-jeff-whitlock]]

### Competitive positioning per [[Jeff Whitlock]]

- Fathom is the most similar competitor to [[Grain]] in approach (video-based, well-designed product)
- Bigger company, growing faster: "They've grown faster than us. We're doing great. We're profitable. We're growing, but they're a bigger company and they're growing faster."
- Jeff believes Grain has the better product: "I think our product's better product, but they made some better business decisions than us to grow faster."
- Fathom is focused on in-product experiences (deals dashboards, etc.) while Grain is betting on being the data/meeting layer for [[Agentic AI]]
- Jeff's read: "I'm not sure they're fully embracing that strategy as much as we are" regarding the agent-first approach
