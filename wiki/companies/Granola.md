# Granola

Competitor to [[Grain]] in the meeting notetaking space. Local-capture-only product. Raised significant funding (~$100M office referenced). Based in London.

## Update 2026-02-23 from [[2026-02-23_strategy-session]]

Team discussed Granola's positioning. They appear to be focusing on the product vertical, which the team found to be "a very strange strategic decision." Their logo rebrand was panned. They added HubSpot integration.

[[Jeff Whitlock]] noted Granola provided a "really great in-meeting experience" with their notepad but "didn't do the follow-up part" as well as [[Fathom]].

## Update 2026-04-23 from [[2026-04-23_pre-board-conversation-april-2026-2]]

[[Mike Adams]] expressed frustration that Granola's founder blew him off when he reached out about a potential meeting in London:

> "I have a bit of a chip on my shoulder of this granola guy blowing me off... I want to kick Granola's ass."

Mike estimated their fundraising means the founder would need a $5B exit for meaningful returns, which he doesn't believe exists.

Jeff: "I wouldn't want to be granola CEO right now... how's he gonna sell for $5 billion?"

## Update 2026-05-15 from [[2026-05-15_strategy-session-5-15-26-capture-and-sharing]]

Referenced as validation of local-capture-first approach. Their success with local-only recording supports [[Grain]]'s direction of investing more in desktop capture over bots.
