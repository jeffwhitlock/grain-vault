# Voice of the Customer

A proposed shared [[Knowledge Graph System]] vault that aggregates customer feedback from all channels into a wiki organized by themes and topics. Endorsed by [[Jeff Whitlock]] for development.

## Update 2026-05-15 from [[2026-05-15_agentic-workflows]]

### Proposal from [[Mike Adams]]

Mike proposed building a shared, automated Voice of the Customer vault:

> "It'd be really interesting to create a processing flow like this, especially that's automated and ideally remote hosted, that is just creating a voice of the customer vault that we could all input to and point to."

Vision:
- Inputs: support channels, sales calls, research, [[Grain]] recordings
- Processing: automated compile into wiki articles organized by themes and topics
- Output: a graph that any team member can query for customer context when making product, marketing, or sales decisions

> "Breaking that into a wiki is not only valuable for just reading the wiki... but building a graph at a more detailed level for the voices of the customer is my first thought of something that could be really valuable as a shared asset."

[[Jeff Whitlock]]'s response: "Let's build it."

### Relation to existing work

Mike is already doing this individually with his vault's customer-facing clusters. The proposal is to formalize it as a shared team resource. [[Grain MCP]] would be a primary input source.
