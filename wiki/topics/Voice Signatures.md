# Voice Signatures

A planned [[Grain]] feature to identify individual speakers using voice biometrics, improving [[Diarization]] quality. Critical for the [[Grain MCP]] to deliver properly attributed transcripts.

## Update 2026-05-05 from [[2026-05-05_q2-2026-board-follow-up]]

Listed as a Q2 planned product project. [[Jeff Whitlock]] argued it is critical for MCP value:

> "Voice signatures are critical for the promise of what MCP wants to do. I was using the MCP today to analyze our board meeting and it's just one massive hunk of text in a markdown file."

Also plays into the entity memory system: knowing who said what helps build per-person models.

[[Jake Adams]] raised biometric legality concerns. [[Mike Adams]] pushed back on letting legal concerns stall the project: "We're not a big Japan company. If any problem, we'll find out about it."

Jeff noted the approach would be scoped to per-workspace voice profiles, not universal cross-workspace identification.
