# PLG Growth

[[Grain]]'s product-led growth motion, including activation, conversion, and expansion. See also [[PQL Pipeline]] and [[Enterprise Expansion]].

## Update 2026-04-29 from [[2026-04-29_account-upgrade-audits]]

[[Mike Adams]] conducted an account-level forensic audit and concluded the self-serve upgrade model is fundamentally incoherent. Most conversions happen at 1-2 seats. The upgrade flow doesn't clearly communicate value. Competitors like [[Fireflies]] and Otter are far more aggressive on growth tactics.

See [[PQL Pipeline]] for the detailed framework developed to address this.

## Update 2026-05-01 from [[2026-05-01_demos]]

Jeff reported that activation rates are higher on local/desktop capture compared to bot capture in the new onboarding experiment. This supports the direction of defaulting new users to desktop capture.
