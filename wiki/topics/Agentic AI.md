# Agentic AI

The paradigm of AI agents that autonomously execute multi-step workflows using tools, APIs, and context. Central to [[Grain Strategy]] and the company's go-to-market positioning as of May 2026.

## Update 2026-05-15 from [[2026-05-15_agentic-workflows]]

### Grain's positioning

[[Jeff Whitlock]] framed agentic AI as a transformational technology that should change how companies work, not just bolt onto existing processes:

> "Agentic technology, agentic AI is a transformational technology. It should not just be bolted on to everything you do today, but should actually make you rethink how your fundamental production processes and how you work."

Jeff is building an analysis of major transformational technologies and the frameworks companies used to respond to them. Will present at the next all-hands.

### Three pillars of value

Jeff articulated [[Grain]]'s three fundamental pillars (from the [[Carlos Almonte]] call):
1. **Universal capture** — making it easy to capture everything; more investment coming
2. **Data enrichment** — [[Knowledge Graph System]] for cross-meeting context, [[Voice Signatures]] for better diarization
3. **Agent-ready data** — structured, rich data delivered efficiently to agents

> "The real value is making it super easy so structured and rich data can be given efficiently to agents to do the work."

### Vision statement

> "Meetings are the interface for work in the world of agentic AI because it's how humans share ideas. It's how decisions are made. It's how alignment happens. And so every meeting has a bunch of knowledge that is context for the work that needs to get done. Our vision is to be the meeting layer for all the agentic work." — [[Jeff Whitlock]]

## Update 2026-05-15 from [[2026-05-15_agentic-workflows]] (internal practices)
