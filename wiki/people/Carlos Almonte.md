# Carlos Almonte

[[Grain]] power user and affiliate partner based in South Florida. Runs a consulting practice serving both for-profit and nonprofit clients. Active user of the [[Grain MCP]] integration with Claude.

## Update 2026-05-15 from [[2026-05-15_carlos-almonte-and-jeff-whitlock]]

### MCP feedback

Carlos is enthusiastic about the [[Grain MCP]]. Connected it to both his IDE and the web version of Claude. Used it to bulk-organize recordings into playlists by client domain:

> "Claude, all my clients that have this domain, put into this playlist. I clicked submit and I walked away. I came back five minutes later, like all done."

Key feature requests for the MCP:
- **Audit tool (read):** "Could I invoke Grain and be like, give me a runner report on how many people have access to my recordings? Like just give me an audit." Wants visibility into which recordings are shared and with whom.
- **Access management (write):** Ability to change recording permissions through the MCP. Per-client security rules so sensitive client recordings are locked down automatically by domain.
- **Delete capability:** Mentioned wanting to delete playlists from MCP but acknowledged the security risk.

### Sharing model concerns

Carlos surfaced a significant pain point around [[Grain Sharing Model]] defaults:
- Did not realize all recordings were visible to his entire team by default
- Had to get [[Grain]] support to retroactively lock down every recording he owned
- Created a dummy account on his own plan to audit what team members could see
- Concerned about sensitive client conversations being exposed to team members

Also flagged the default "send recording to all participants" as risky:

> "I got so uncomfortable the first time it happened because I didn't say anything, but I'm like, Oh my gosh, I can't believe this went to the clients."

Proposed a middle ground: notify participants by default, but require explicit permission grant for recording access.

### Competitive positioning

Asked [[Jeff Whitlock]] to position [[Grain]] vs competitors. Has clients on [[Otter]] and [[Fireflies]]. Clients who received Grain recordings loved the video component (Otter is transcription-only).

### Sales pipeline

Planning to sign up 10-15 users at a client agency onto [[Grain]] in the next month. Building a feedback loop system: Grain -> [[HubSpot]] -> product lead's IDE, using [[N8N]] for automation. Already has an affiliate code for the for-profit side; requested one for nonprofit clients as well.
